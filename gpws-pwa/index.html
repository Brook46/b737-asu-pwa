PFD per FCOM 10.31: attitude (2.5° pitch scale, bank pointer), speed tape with VMO/min-speed bands, altitude tape, VSI, magenta glideslope/localizer, radio-altitude box, and the red PULL UP / WINDSHEAR annunciation. ND terrain colours follow FCOM 15.10.11.

ND terrain colour key (FCOM)
  • Solid red — look-ahead terrain warning
  • Solid amber — look-ahead terrain caution
  • Dotted red — terrain > 2000 ft above you
  • Dotted amber — 500 ft (250 gear-down) below → 2000 ft above
  • Dotted green — 2000 ft below → 500 ft below
  • Black — no significant terrain · magenta — no data

Tap a mode to load a matching scenario — it highlights, drives the displays, lights the annunciations and plays its aural. Tap again to clear.
